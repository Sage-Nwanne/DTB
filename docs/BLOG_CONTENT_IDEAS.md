## ‚úçÔ∏è Content Creation Workflow

### **Step 1: Choose Topic** (5 min)
- Pick from this list
- Check search volume (Google it, see what comes up)
- Make sure you can write 800+ words on it

### **Step 2: Research** (15 min)
- Google the topic
- Read top 3 results
- Note what they cover
- Find gaps you can fill

### **Step 3: Outline** (10 min)
- Use the template from BLOG_POST_TEMPLATE.md
- List your main points (H2s)
- Add sub-points (H3s)

### **Step 4: Write** (60-90 min)
- Follow your outline
- Don't edit while writing
- Just get words on the page

### **Step 5: Edit** (30 min)
- Read out loud
- Cut fluff
- Add examples
- Check for typos

### **Step 6: Optimize** (15 min)
- Add keywords naturally
- Write meta description
- Add alt text to images
- Add internal links

### **Step 7: Publish & Promote** (30 min)
- Schedule post
- Share on social media
- Email to your list
- Submit to Google Search Console

**Total Time**: 3-4 hours per post
