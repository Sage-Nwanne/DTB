About DTB

We're a small team obsessed with building systems that work. No fluff. No excuses. Just results.

We exist to help small teams punch above their weight. In a world where enterprise tools are expensive and generic, we build custom solutions that actually fit your business.

Every project we take on is an opportunity to prove that great design and smart automation can transform a business. We don't just build websites or automate tasks—we build competitive advantages.

Our goal is simple: help you grow faster, work smarter, and compete like you're 10x bigger than you are.

This isn't just our tagline—it's our philosophy. We design with intention, automate with precision, and deliver breakthroughs that matter.

Our Journey

2020

The Spark

DTB began with a simple idea: small teams deserve the same level of design and technology as big brands. What started as late-night builds for local businesses quickly grew into a mission to make digital excellence accessible.

2021

From Projects to Purpose

We partnered with local restaurants, churches, and creators—learning what truly drives growth in small organizations. Every launch sharpened our craft and deepened our belief that great systems start with great people.

2022

The Breakthrough

We stepped beyond websites and into automation and AI—designing intelligent tools that help teams save time, stay consistent, and scale faster. DTB became more than a studio; it became a systems partner.

2023

Building Partnerships

Our focus shifted toward long-term collaboration. Through white-label work, consulting, and technical partnerships, DTB began powering other agencies and entrepreneurs behind the scenes.

2024

Designed to Scale

Today, DTB helps clients across industries build smarter operations and stronger brands. We're still driven by the same mission—to turn ideas into systems that break through barriers and build lasting impact.
