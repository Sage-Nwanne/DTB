Our Journey

2020

The Spark

DTB began with a simple idea: small teams deserve the same level of design and technology as big brands. What started as late-night builds for local businesses quickly grew into a mission to make digital excellence accessible.

2021

From Projects to Purpose

We partnered with local restaurants, churches and creators, learning what truly drives growth in small organizations. Every launch sharpened our craft and deepened our belief that great systems start with great people.

2022

The Breakthrough

We stepped beyond websites and into automation and AI, designing intelligent tools that help teams save time, stay consistent and scale faster. DTB became more than a studio; it became a systems partner.

2023

Building Partnerships

Our focus shifted toward long-term collaboration. Through white-label work, consulting and technical partnerships, DTB began powering other agencies and entrepreneurs behind the scenes.

2024

Designed to Scale

DTB helped clients across industries build smarter operations and stronger brands. We remained driven by the same mission: to turn ideas into systems that break through barriers and build lasting impact.

2025

Trusted & Respected

Our clients saw real success: increased engagement, streamlined operations and measurable growth. Through consistent delivery and genuine partnership, we've built a reputation rooted in trust and results. As we look toward 2026, we're committed to maintaining this momentum, deepening our expertise and continuing to deliver breakthrough solutions that drive lasting impact.
