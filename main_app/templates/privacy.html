Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 2025

1. Introduction

Designed To Breakthrough LLC ("DTB," "we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard personal information from our clients, website visitors, and partners in accordance with Pennsylvania state and federal privacy laws.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of data:

  • Personal Information: name, email address, phone number, business name, and billing details provided during consultations, contracts, or contact form submissions.
  • Project Information: brand assets, digital files, credentials, and other materials provided by clients for service execution.
  • Technical Data: website usage metrics, browser type, IP address, and analytics data for internal optimization and performance tracking.
  • Payment Information: payment confirmations (e.g., Zelle, PayPal, Venmo, CashApp) are processed securely and never stored on DTB's servers.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected data for:

  • Providing contracted services and managing client projects.
  • Communication regarding project updates, invoices, and customer support.
  • Marketing and portfolio representation (consistent with our Client Services Agreement).
  • Security, analytics, and service improvement.

We do not sell, lease, or trade your data to third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain client information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, comply with legal obligations, and maintain operational records.

5. Information Sharing

We may share limited data with:

  • Authorized contractors or team members under confidentiality agreements.
  • Third-party vendors (e.g., hosting, analytics, automation tools) solely for service delivery.
  • Legal authorities, when required by law or to protect DTB's rights or property.

6. Data Security

We employ industry-standard encryption, secure storage, and restricted access protocols. While no digital system is fully immune to breach, we take all reasonable precautions to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

7. Client Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of inaccurate data (where applicable).
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.

Requests can be made to inquire@dtbsolutions.tech

8. Portfolio and Marketing Use

By engaging DTB's services, you acknowledge and consent to our right to reference, discuss, or display completed work (including visuals, descriptions, and results) across DTB's platforms—without the need for prior consent, written or otherwise—unless otherwise restricted by a separate confidentiality agreement.

9. Changes to This Policy

DTB may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of our services or website following notice of such updates constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.

10. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries or data requests, contact:
